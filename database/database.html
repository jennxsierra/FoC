Database

A database is like a digital filing cabinet where information is stored in an organized way. It helps computer programs find, change, and use data efficiently. Databases are used in many applications, like websites and apps, to store and manage information, such as user accounts and product details. They come in different types, depending on the kind of data and how it's structured.
